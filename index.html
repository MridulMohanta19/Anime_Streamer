Demon Slayer

Tanjirou Kamado is responsible for supporting his family,
living in poverty on a remote mountain. He seeks refuge in a stranger's home and
is warned of the presence of carnivorous demons
in the night forest. After returning home, his family has been murdered and
his sister Nezuko is cursed into a bloodthirsty demon.
Tanjirou vows to avenge his family and take care of Nezuko,
joining the mysterious Demon Slayer Corps to destroy demons and protect her sister's humanity.
